List of commands in the action bar
Go to...: Select a room you want to move your character to, provided the room is available.
Attack: Choose which area of the enemy you want to attack. Strike the enemy weak spot for increased damage.
Explore: Use to search a room for items, pick up found items, or drop items from inventory.
Help: Get help for the game options. 
Game Options: Use to save your current progress, load a previous game, or start a new game.
